After months of development, I'm excited to share my latest project: Orbit AI - an intelligent career development platform that's revolutionizing how job seekers approach their career journey.

🎯 **Key Features:**
• AI-Powered Resume Analyzer with ATS scoring
• Personalized Career Roadmaps & Skill Tracking  
• Smart Job Matching based on user profiles
• Interactive AI Tutor for career guidance
